# Aurumix V3 Summary

## What It Is, In Plain Terms

Aurumix is a gold savings programme delivered as a token.

Investors save monthly. Every dollar buys physical gold held in a Dubai LBMA vault. They get AURX tokens that represent their grams.

On top of the gold, three things make it more than a storage locker:

- A credit facility so they borrow against their gold instead of selling it
- A dividend that pays them a share of Aurumix's profits
- A family system that passes the gold to their children automatically

Dubai, VARA regulated. Target investor is the long-term NRI and diaspora saver, closer to an LIC subscriber than a crypto trader.

---

## What Changed From The Old Model

This is the important part, since the last version was different.

Old model:
- Every dollar split 70% gold and 30% into an algorithmic growth pool
- Token price was set by a formula the company controlled
- Upside came from the algo pool markup

New model:
- 100% of every dollar buys gold. No split. No algo pool. No formula.
- Token price is simply the gold price. Nothing else.
- Upside now comes from gold appreciation plus a profit dividend

Three things are brand new in this version:
- The ICS Dividend (a share of company profit paid to investors)
- The Family Portfolio and Digital Will (inheritance built in)
- The token is now 1 token = 0.01 gram of gold, pegged exactly

## The Mental Model

You save in gold, gram by gram.

Instead of that gold sitting idle, you can:
- Borrow against it
- Earn a dividend on it
- Pass it to your family

Four promises: accumulate, borrow, earn, inherit.

## Chronology

### 1. Investor Joins
- Downloads the Aurumix Circle app, completes KYC
- Sets up a monthly SIP ($20 minimum, $75 target)
- Picks a lock-in period from 6 months to 25 years
- Longer lock-in earns more rewards later, including a bigger dividend

### 2. Money Becomes Gold
- Mining Events run monthly in Years 1 and 2, then fortnightly from Year 3
- At each event, a small entry fee is taken (2 to 5%)
- 100% of the rest buys physical gold at that day's LBMA price
- Investor receives AURX at 0.01 gram per token

### 3. Gold Receipt And Gram Counter
- Each purchase is recorded as a Gold Receipt in the investor's name
- The app shows a gram counter that grows with every payment

### 4. Confirmed SIP Status
- After 6 consecutive events, allocation becomes guaranteed
- This is the milestone every new investor is working toward

### 5. Building ICS
- ICS (Investor Conviction Score) rises the longer and bigger they invest
- Built from: continuity (the main driver), lock-in length, tenure, holding value, referrals (capped), Masterclass, and family activity
- One score, four payoffs: event priority, spot access, credit limit, dividend share
- Investors climb seven tiers, from Green Member up to Sovereign Member

### 6. Spot Events
- After each SIP event, a smaller Spot Event opens
- Its size is a ratio of the SIP event (20, 30 or 40%, still to be confirmed)
- Only higher-ICS investors can grab this extra gold, with no lock-in
- This is the earned reward for loyalty

### 7. Borrow Instead Of Sell
- Credit facility activates after 6 events
- Investor borrows USDT against their gold, gold stays in the vault
- Borrowing limit rises with tier: Gold 50%, Platinum 70%, Sovereign up to 110% of gold value
- A Visa or Mastercard Gold Card spends the credit anywhere

### 8. Annual Custody Fee
- Once a year a small amount of gold is taken in grams to cover vault costs (0.8 to 1%)
- The only thing that reduces the gram counter

### 9. ICS Dividend
- Once a year, 15 to 20% of Aurumix profit is shared with investors
- Weighted by ICS score times investment value
- The top 10% of investors take 80% of the pool, the rest share 20%
- Lapsed investors get nothing until they rebuild their score
- Paid in USDT or added as more gold grams

### 10. Family Portfolio And Digital Will
- Investor splits their gold into named sub-portfolios for family
- Each has its own dashboard, credit, and dividend
- A Digital Will transfers each share automatically at a set trigger (a date, a life event, or a condition)

### 11. Secondary Market
- Around Year 2 the token lists on exchanges
- It can trade at a modest premium over gold because the credit and dividend rights travel with it
- This is not promised, just the natural pricing

### 12. Exit Or Renew
- At lock-in expiry they can redeem at the gold price, sell, or renew
- Leaving starts ICS decay and shrinks the dividend, so expiry is built to be a renewal moment

## How Aurumix Earns

- Entry fee on each payment
- Annual gold custody fee, taken in grams
- Credit facility fees

It shares 15 to 20% of the profit from these back to investors as the dividend.

Distribution is through a 3-tier agent network, hard-capped at three levels, paid out of fee revenue, never charged to the investor.

## The Targets

- Year 1: 500 active SIP investors
- Year 3: 10,000 to 14,000
- Year 10: 60,000 to 100,000
- No hard cap on token supply. It grows only as fast as gold can be bought and vaulted.

## Summary

It is gold dollar-cost-averaging, with a credit line, a profit dividend, and built-in inheritance, wrapped in a token.

The token price is now just the gold price, nothing more.

Retention comes from three things working together: the lock-in, the dividend you lose if you leave, and the family wealth you are building.
